About UNL Baja SAE

The University of Nebraska Lincoln’ s chapter, Husker Racing, has been in existence for over 20 years and is one of the most decorated and acclaimed teams in the Midwest region. Our team attends at least one of the three U.S. competitions hosted. Husker Racing’s success is built on the passion and dedication of the members throughout the yearlong, intensive design and build seasons.

Husker Racing provides a hands-on experience that students cannot gain inside of a lecture hall; this experience serves to help develop students with well-rounded professional skills in various aspects of the engineering industry. Equally important, students actively build non-technical skills vital to professional engineering: program/timeline management, budgeting, interpersonal communication, and conflict resolution.

At the University of Nebraska-Lincoln (UNL), Husker Racing accepts all students interested, regardless of major, year, or background.
